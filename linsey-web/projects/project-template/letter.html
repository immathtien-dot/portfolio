Invitation Project

This is an invitation birthday for Ellie.

This invitation was designed as a digital experience rather than a traditional printed card. Instead of sending physical invitations, I created a small website that allows guests to access birthday information through interaction. The invitation is not just information, but also an experience.

It combines creativity and technology to create a modern, accessible, and memorable birthday invitation for everyone who receives it.
